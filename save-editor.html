Trimps Save Editor

Edit a Trimps save entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. In Trimps, go to Settings and Export to get your save code, edit it here, then paste the new code back into Import.

Back up first. Copy your current Export code somewhere safe before importing an edited one, so you can restore if something goes wrong.

Paste a save and press Decode to begin.

2. Find and edit values

Type part of a field name or path to find it. Some fields carry a note:

  • linked: editing a perk's level automatically recomputes its spent helium or radon, and editing an equipment level automatically adjusts your army attack or health totals — both exactly as the game itself would.
  • helium and radon stay consistent: owned, leftover, lifetime total, and perk spending are one linked set. Edit any of them and the others recompute — raising the lifetime total puts the difference straight into your spendable pool.
  • equipment prestige is safe to edit alone: the game recomputes that equipment's stats and cost from prestige when the save loads.
  • derived: the game recomputes this on load, so your edit will not stick. Edit the source field instead.
  • warnings: changing your zone leaves a stale enemy grid, and over-filling jobs past your population is flagged when you edit.

Jump to zone

Sets your current zone and zone record, and unlocks what a natural run would have by then, read from the game's own drop tables: the portal, broken planet, formations, automation, jobs, buildings, equipment, foremen, and every one-shot feature book in either universe. Repeatable book upgrades (Coordination, TrainTacular, Efficiency, Gigastation, and the like) are made available to buy in the right quantities — purchase them in-game after editing resources; Coordination also needs trimp housing. It never removes anything, so jumping low is harmless.
